What is a verified account?

Below, you can apply for an account verification. A verified account can post verified posts- A verified post will be considered factual, for example recent research paper, an experiment log or a scientific article.
Requirements:

  • At least 2 months old account.
  • At least 5 posts, none of which reported for breaking the guidelines
  • NCSF recognized certification or degree

In the application: State your full name, age, username, and attach photos of degrees and/or certificates
